# 📖 Project Overview

This project was developed for the **FP20 Analytics × ZoomCharts Mini Challenge**, where the objective was to transform pharmacy sales data into an interactive business intelligence solution.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by combining sales, profitability, product performance, pharmacy operations, and geographic analysis into a single interactive report.

Business users can quickly identify growth opportunities, monitor KPIs, compare regional performance, evaluate product profitability, and support strategic decision-making through interactive visual analytics.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This dashboard helps business leaders answer key business questions, including:

- Which countries generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which pharmacy locations consistently outperform others?
- Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
- How do branded and generic medicines compare?
- Which products deliver the highest profitability?
- How is the business performing month over month?

# 💡 Business Insights

## 💰 Overall Business Performance

- Generated **€8.63M** in total revenue.
- Achieved **€2.42M** in total margin.
- Sold over **445K units**.
- Revenue, margin, cost, and units sold all increased by approximately **5% Month-over-Month**, demonstrating stable business growth.

---

## 💊 Category Performance

- Prescription medicines generated the highest revenue.
- OTC and Wellness products contributed significantly to overall sales.
- Medical Devices produced the lowest revenue, highlighting an opportunity for future business expansion.

---

## 🌍 Geographic Performance

- Germany emerged as the highest-performing market.
- France and Italy followed closely in revenue generation.
- Austria, Poland, and Spain showed comparatively lower performance and present potential growth opportunities.

---

## 🏥 Pharmacy Performance

- Urban pharmacies consistently outperformed suburban and rural pharmacies.
- Multiple HealthPoint pharmacy branches ranked among the highest revenue-generating locations.

---

## 📦 Inventory Insights

- Medium-sized storage facilities generated the highest revenue.
- Efficient inventory utilization contributed more to profitability than larger storage capacity.

---

## 💵 Product Profitability

- Branded medicines generated the highest revenue.
- Generic medicines maintained strong profit margins despite lower sales volumes.
- Several prescription medicines consistently ranked among the most profitable products.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Expand inventory and marketing efforts in Germany, France, and Italy.
- Investigate customer demand in lower-performing markets.
- Promote Medical Devices through bundled offers.
- Increase awareness of high-margin generic medicines.
- Replicate best practices from top-performing pharmacies.
- Continue monitoring profitability alongside revenue for sustainable growth.
